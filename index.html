Prompt Engineering Guide for Real Estate Professionals

Mastering the art of writing prompts is the key to getting the most out of AI tools. Use this guide as a reference to achieve the best results in all your tasks, from content creation to image generation. Remember, the more specific your request, the better the response.

Best Practices: The Fail-Proof Formula

  • **Target Audience:** Start your prompt by describing the audience. (e.g., "For a young family...")
  • **Clear Task:** Define exactly what you want. (e.g., "Write a property description.")
  • **Essential Data:** Include all relevant property data. (e.g., "3-bed, Cascais, ‚Ç¨400,000, sea view...")
  • **Tone and Style:** Specify the tone (formal, enthusiastic, professional). (e.g., "In a warm and welcoming tone.")
  • **Constraints (Negative Prompt):** Indicate what you do not want included. (e.g., "Do not include technical jargon.")

Marketing & Copywriting

Transform property features into stories that sell. Use prompts to create persuasive descriptions, social media posts, or ad copy.

Example Prompt 1: Property Description

"Write an ad description for a 3-bedroom apartment in Oeiras. The target audience is young families. Key features: renovated kitchen, private garden, close to schools and parks. The tone should be welcoming and highlight quality of life. Add a call to action to schedule a visit."

Example Prompt 2: Instagram Post

"Create an Instagram post about a 2-bedroom apartment in Cascais for investors. Focus on its proximity to the city center, rental potential, and return on investment. Use concise language, emojis, and relevant hashtags (#RealEstateInvestment #Cascais)."

Image Generation (Prompt-to-Image)

Bring empty spaces to life or create lifestyle visuals that capture buyers' imagination. The prompts below help generate photorealistic images for virtual staging or marketing.

Example Prompt 1: Virtual Staging

"Create a photorealistic image of a vacant living room in a 3-bedroom apartment in Lisbon, with a minimalist Scandinavian design style. Include a grey sofa, a wooden coffee table, and a tall plant in the corner. Focus on the natural light coming in from the large window."

Example Prompt 2: Lifestyle

"Generate an image of a young Portuguese family enjoying a barbecue in the garden of a villa in Quinta da Marinha. The scene is in the late afternoon with soft sunlight, and details should include an elegant garden design and an infinity pool. Photorealistic."

Image Generation (Image-to-Image)

Transform an existing property photo by changing lighting, adding elements, or adjusting the style. This is ideal for quick demonstrations or improving photos that aren't perfect.

Example Prompt 1: Change Daylight

"Based on this exterior photo of a property taken during the day, transform the image to show a cozy evening atmosphere. Add soft lights in the garden and turn on the interior lights to show an inviting ambiance. Photorealistic style."

Example Prompt 2: Add Decor

"Based on this photo of an empty living room, add minimalist and contemporary furniture and decor. Place a grey sectional sofa, a glass coffee table, and a soft rug. The image should look photorealistic and move-in ready."

Video Generation (Prompt-to-Video)

Create property trailers or dynamic clips for social media. Describe the action, setting, and filming style to get the best result.

Example Prompt 1: Villa Virtual Tour

"Create a video that follows a smooth, 'first-person' walkthrough of a modern three-bedroom villa in Aroeira. The camera starts in the living room, moves to the kitchen, down the hallway, and ends in the garden with the swimming pool. The lighting is natural and soft."
